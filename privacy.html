Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

At DorkSearch PRO, accessible from https://dorksearch.pro, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by DorkSearch PRO and how we use it.

1. General Information & Data Collection

DorkSearch PRO operates primarily as a client-side tool. We do not store your search queries, target domains, or generated dorks on our servers. All processing happens locally in your web browser.

2. Google AdSense & DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is a third-party vendor on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to dorksearch.pro and other sites on the internet.

However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Advertising Partners Privacy Policies

Third-party ad servers or ad networks uses technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on DorkSearch PRO, which are sent directly to users' browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit.

Note that DorkSearch PRO has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

4. Log Files & Analytics

DorkSearch PRO follows a standard procedure of using log files and Google Analytics 4 (GA4). These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

5. GDPR Data Protection Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
